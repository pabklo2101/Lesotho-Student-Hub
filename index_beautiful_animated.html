    About

    Free platform for Basotho students.

    🇱🇸 Lesotho Student Hub is a free all-in-one study platform for Basotho students. It brings together study resources, LGCSE exam access, quizzes, planning tools, focus sessions, and community support ideas.

    This version keeps the existing project idea but fixes broken UI behaviour, improves mobile responsiveness, adds local saving, and protects the app from missing external files.
