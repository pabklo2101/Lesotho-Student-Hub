ℹ️ About Lesotho Student Hub

🇱🇸 A free, open‑source platform built for Basotho students.

To further improve this hub, consider adding these files to your repository:

  • sw.js – Service worker for offline PWA support.
  • manifest.json – PWA manifest for installability.
  • server.py / server.js – A tiny backend to serve files with dynamic features (e.g., using Flask or Express).
  • file_list.json – List of your own local study files (PDFs, notes) to appear as downloadable cards.
  • Free backend hosting: Vercel, Netlify, Render, or PythonAnywhere – all offer free tiers.
